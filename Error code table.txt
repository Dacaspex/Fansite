6 - Something went wrong while checking your log in credentials (alert, redirect -> index)
7 - You can't register while logged in (warning, redirect -> index)
8 - Successfully logged in (success)
9 - Password wass incorrect (warning)
10 - The username doesn't exist (yet) on this website (warning)
